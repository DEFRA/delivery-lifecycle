This delivery lifecycle shows how services are delivered in Defra.

It is a guide for anyone delivering services on behalf of Defra, whether inside or outside of the department.

It exists to ensure effective delivery of services through:

  • A shared understanding of the expected ways of working, so that everyone involved with service delivery is able to get their job done.
  • A common vocabulary to be used so as to avoid errors and omissions through misunderstandings and miscommunications.

ALPHA This guide is currently just a prototype to be used to get feedback.
