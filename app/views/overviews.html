Defra Service Delivery Lifecycle Overviews

All Services will be delivered in a series of phases as defined by the Service Manual: Discovery, Alpha, Beta and Live.

This section provides an overview of how these phases apply to different types of delivery.

The lifecycle is described in terms of the interactions between teams. The overviews below are provided for each of the three different types of team involved in delivery: vertical, horizontal and functional.

Vertical Teams Overview

The vertical teams overview identifies the interactions with multiple horizontal and functional teams.

It focuses on:

  • Interactions with teams that are delivering cross-cutting services who may be at different phases in their own delivery lifecycle.
  • Interactions with functional teams providing operational support throughout the lifecycle.
  • Governance and assurance that take place during the delivery phases.

Horizontal Teams Overview

The horizontal teams overview identifies the interactions with multiple vertical and functional teams.

It focuses on:

  • Interactions with teams that are delivering line of business services who may be at different phases in their own delivery lifecycle.
  • Interactions with other cross-cutting service teams who may be at different phases in their own delivery lifecycle.
  • Interactions with functional teams providing operational support, in particular the continuous improvement activities of a live service.
  • How governance is applied across the line of business services that the horizontal service will support.

Functional Teams Overviews

The overviews for functional teams provide a summary of the interactions with multiple horizontal and vertical teams.

They focus on:

  • Governance and assurance that take place during the delivery phases.
  • Early contact points with service teams.
  • Identification of resource demand from operational teams.
