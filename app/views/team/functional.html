Functional teams

Functional teams provide capabilities that are required by the multidisciplinary service teams, but these capabilities are coalesced into central teams for reasons of efficiency and effectiveness.

The size and existince of this type of team will depend on the overall number of services, as well as organisational considerations.

This delivery lifecycle does not define organisational structures, but it does identify a number of conceptual functional teams that work with service teams.

Over time, this picture will change and these conceptual teams will be formed into actual organisational structures. This information will be updated to reflect that but for now the names of people and groups within Defra are provided where available.
