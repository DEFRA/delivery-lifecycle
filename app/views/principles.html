Defra service delivery lifecycle principles

The design of the service delivery lifecycle is based on a number of principles that are derived from the Defra Target Operating Model, the Defra Enterprise Architecture Principles and the Government Digital Service Standard.

These principles are already well established, so the purpose of this section is to identify how they apply to the service delivery lifecycle.

Common governance

A single, common governance mechanism will be used across all service delivery.

Sources: EA - Architectural Management and Governance, EA - Business Alignment, EA - IT Investment

This means that we need a service delivery lifecycle that is driven by governance mechanisms and can use those mechanisms to ensure delivery.

Additionally, all applications will need to have strong business ownership, not just those that are closely aligned to business functions.

Digital by default

Defra services will move to digital channels as the primary delivery route.

Sources: Defra TOM

The implication of this is that we need a service delivery lifecycle that is focused on delivering digital applications.

Buy first

We will seek to derive maximum value by Buying ready-made applications rather than Building our own where possible

Sources: EA - IT Investment, EA - Cloud First, EA App - SaaS and Commodity First, EA Tech - Common Platforms, GDS - Use open standards and common platforms

We need a service delivery lifecycle that is designed for Services that will comprise both Bought and Built components.

Digital service standard

We will apply the Government Digital Service Standard to the delivery of all services

Sources: Digital standards...?

We need a service delivery lifecycle that elaborates rather than repeats the Service Manual.

Managed

We will always have the appropriate level of Operation Support around our Applications

Sources: EA - Business Continuity, EA App - Application Service Delivery, GDS - Test the end-to-end service, GDS - Make a plan for being offline, GDS - Collect performance data

We need a service delivery lifecycle that includes Operation Support throughout.

Common bought components need to be managed with the same level of Operation Support as services.

This includes having strong business ownership of all applications and data sets, not just those that are closely aligned to business functions.

Iterative

We will always iterate our Applications to ensure continuous delivery of value

Sources: EA App - Iterative Delivery, EA Tech - Build for Automation, GDS - Iterate and improve frequently

We need a service delivery lifecycle that enables safe iteration.
