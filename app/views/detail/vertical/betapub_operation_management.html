Public beta operation management

Following a successful assessment the Service Team will open up their service for general use in a Public Beta.

The Operation Management function will take on ownership of some aspects of the service at this point, generally the day to day running of the applications and the engagement with the Operation Support and Support Design functions.

Without this, the Service Team would need to retain people to perform this function. Unless it is an unusually large service then this is unlikely to be economical.

Teams

These are the teams that are involved in this interaction and what they want to achieve.

Service team

"I want my service to be supported in Public Beta."

"I want to scale down the team effort."

Operation management team

"I want to know what the joint responsibilities with the Service Team will be."

"I want to correctly scale my team to support the service."

"I want to move to a regular, predictable support rhythm."

Objectives

The objective of this interaction is to ensure that the service can be continuously improved without having to maintain a full service team.

Activities

The service team and operation management function will work together on the following activities.

Knowledge transfer

Transfer of service knowledge from the Service Team to the Operations Management function.

Continuous change

Maintaining continuous service change.

Timing

This interaction will started at the beginning of the Public Beta.
