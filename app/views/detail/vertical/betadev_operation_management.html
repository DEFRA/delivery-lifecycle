Beta operation management

During Beta the Service Team will iteratively build the applications that form their service.

The Operation Management function will provide development equivalents of their services and ready their services for the Private and Public Betas.

Without this, the operational applications will not be ready for continuous deployment and so Private and Public Betas will not be possible.

Teams

These are the teams that are involved in this interaction and what they want to achieve.

Service team

"I want to iteratively build on shared services."

Operation management team

"I want to manage risks associated with development activity."

Objectives

The objective of this interaction is to ensure that the service team are able to include shared services in their development.

Activities

The service team and operation management function will work together on the following activities.

Build development service

Configure and use the development service for Beta or create one if it doesn't already exist.

Configuration may be performed by the Service Team or by the appropriate Operation Support function.

The operation management function will provide a delivery mechanism for continuous deployment.

Prepare the production service

Establish the introduction mechanism for the production service.

Timing

This interaction should be started at the beginning of Beta. This is to allow the development to occur.
