Information assurance

During Alpha, the Service Team will build and trial a number of temporary prototypes and demonstrators, some of which may contain sensitive information or intellectual property that would need to be protected.

The Information Assurance function can assist the Service Team in identifying this information and ensuring that it is appropriately protected.

Without this, the service team is liable to under- or over-protect the relevant information, causing unnecessary extra work or, in the worst case, security breaches.

Teams

These are the teams that are involved in this interaction and what they want to achieve.

Service team

"I want to build, trial, demonstrate and dispose of prototypes quickly."

Information assurance team

"I want to ensure that security risks associated with prototyping activity are appropriately mitigated."

Objectives

The objective of this interaction is to maintain the security of prototypes and demonstrators.

Activities

The Service Team and Information Assurance function will work together on the following activities.

Threat modelling

Identifying the applicable threats and appropriate controls.

The Information Assurance function will help the Service Team to identify relevant security threats associated with prototyping activity and describe the appropriate controls.

Timing

This interaction should be started early in Alpha. This is so that the team can build in the appropriate controls from the start.
