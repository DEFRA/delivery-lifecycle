Alpha operation management

During Alpha, the Service Team will build and trial a number of temporary prototypes and demonstrators, many of which will be shown directly to customers.

The Operation Management function can assist the Service Team by providing sandboxed equivalents of their services to demonstrate to users.

Without this, the service team will have to implement their own dummy services for Alpha, which will cause delays and could provide misleading information.

Teams

These are the teams that are involved in this interaction and what they want to achieve.

Service team

"I want to iteratively demonstrate shared services."

Operation management team

"I want to manage risks associated with prototyping activity."

Objectives

The objective of this interaction is to ensure that the service team are able to include shared services in their demonstrations to users so that they can make the best decisions about how to proceed in Beta.

Activities

The service team and operation management function will work together on the following activities.

Use sandbox service

Include the Alpha in the sandbox service or create one if it doesn't already exist.

Timing

This interaction should be started early in Alpha. This is to allow the service team to have prioritised their backlog and understood whether they will benefit from getting feedback on shared services.
