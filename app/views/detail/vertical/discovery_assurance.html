Assurance

The Service Team will need to ensure that their Discovery exercise is covering all the areas that are specified by the Service Manual.

The Assurance function can help the Service Team to understand what they should be seeking to achieve from Discovery and to assess whether they have done so.

Without this interaction, the Discovery exercise could waste effort and leave the team unprepared for Alpha.

Teams

These are the teams that are involved in this interaction and what they want to achieve.

Service team

"I want to be sure of the objectives of Discovery and that I will meet them."

"I want to be ready for Alpha."

"I want to be confident in the outcome of Discovery."

Assurance team

"I want to ensure that service teams are doing the right things."

"I want to be confident in the outcome of Discovery."

Objectives

The objective of this interaction is to ensure that when Discovery is complete, all the right things have been done so that we can be confident in the outcome.

Activities

The Service Team and Assurance function will work together on the following activities.

Discovery guidance

The Assurance function provide guidance to the Service Team on what is expected from a Discovery phase.

During Discovery, the Service Team work with the Assurance function to enhance the guidance based on delivery experience.

This guidance includes the basis on which the Discovery will be assessed.

Discovery assessment

The Discovery phase does not have a formal assessment at the end, but assessment of the outcomes of Discovery form part of the spend approval process.

The Assurance function work with the Service Team throughout Discovery to ensure that they comply with the Service Standard.

The Assurance function provide their assessment as part of the business case for Alpha.

Timing

This interaction should be started at the beginning of the Discovery phase.
