Beta operation support

During Beta the Service Team will iteratively build the applications that form their service.

The Operation Support function will provide the necessary platforms, configuration and monitoring capabilities to support development.

They will also supply dedicated environments for Private and Public Beta.

Without this interaction, the Service Team will be unable to develop their Beta.

Teams

These are the teams that are involved in this interaction and what they want to achieve.

Service team

"I want to perform continuous integration and test, deploying to a representative platform to get regular feedback."

Operation support team

"I want to apply appropriate monitoring and controls."

Objectives

The objective of this interaction is to ensure that the service team are able to perform their Beta development.

The Operation support team gain an understanding of the operating characteristics of the applications.

Activities

The service team and operation support function will work together on the following activities.

Provision environments

Stand up environments that are required by the service team through scripted provisioning.

Continuous deployment

Implementing continuous deployment facilities, including promotion from development to production environments.

Manage environments

Monitoring and controlling the environments.

Timing

This interaction should be started at the beginning of Beta. This is to allow the development to occur.
