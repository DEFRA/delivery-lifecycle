Horizontal services in public Beta or Live

During Alpha the Service Team will want to understand and assess the Horizontal Services that they may need to interact with.

For any Horizontal Services that are already in public Beta or Live, the service team need to make use of the common services to demonstrate to their users.

Without this interaction, the service will proceed to Beta with incorrect assumptions and an invalid business case.

Teams

These are the teams that are involved in this interaction and what they want to achieve.

Service team

"I want to know that the horizontal service will work for my Beta."

Horizontal service teams

"I want to maintain service levels for my users."

"I want to plan to support the service team in their Beta."

Objectives

The objective of this interaction is to evaluate whether the horizontal service will work for the users.

It also ensures that the horizontal service will be ready for the vertical service when it moves into Beta.

Activities

The service teams will work together on the following activities.

Demonstrators

Creating demonstrators for user feedback.

The Service Team engage the Horizontal Service team through a service request to deliver demonstration environments and configuration capability.

(We don't currently seem to have a mechanism for doing this.)

Timing

This interaction should be started once the Service Team are ready to begin producing prototypes and demonstrators.
