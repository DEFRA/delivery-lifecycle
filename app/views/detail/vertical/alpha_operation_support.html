Alpha operation support

During Alpha, the Service Team will build and trial a number of temporary prototypes and demonstrators, many of which will be shown directly to customers.

The Operation Support function can assist the Service Team in obtaining the necessary platforms, configuration and light-touch monitoring capabilities.

Without this, the service team will have to procure and manage all the necessary infrastructure for their Alpha, which will cause delays.

Teams

These are the teams that are involved in this interaction and what they want to achieve.

Service team

"I want to build, trial, demonstrate and dispose of prototypes quickly."

Operation support team

"I want to manage risks associated with prototyping activity."

Objectives

The objective of this interaction is to ensure that the service team are able to perform their prototyping in representative environments so that they can make the best decisions about how to proceed in Beta.

Activities

The service team and operation support function will work together on the following activities.

Provision environments

Stand up environments that are required by the service team.

Manage environments

Monitoring and controlling the environments.

Timing

This interaction should be started at the beginning of Alpha. This is to allow time for environments to be provisioned.
