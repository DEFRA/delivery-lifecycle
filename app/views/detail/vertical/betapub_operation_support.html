Public beta operation support

Following a successful assessment the Service Team will open up their service for general use in a Public Beta.

This phase focuses on transitioning from continuous change to continuous improvement.

The Operation Support function will work with the Service Team to assess the performance of the operating environments during the Public Beta and ensure that there is a continuous feedback loop for improvement.

Without this interaction, the Service Team will be unable to transition to a continuous improvement state.

Teams

These are the teams that are involved in this interaction and what they want to achieve.

Service team

"I want my service to be supported in Public Beta."

"I want to identify, understand and resolve issues arising from operating at full scale."

"I want to scale down the team effort."

Operation support team

"I want to get practice supporting the service."

"I want to correctly scale my team to support the service."

"I want to move to a regular, predictable support rhythm."

Objectives

The objective of this interaction is to ensure that the service will continue to be supported during Public Beta in such a way that the service can progress to Live.

Activities

The service team and operation support function will work together on the following activities.

Knowledge transfer

Transfer of service knowledge from the Service Team to the Operation Support function.

In some cases this may involve transfer of people or contracts, most likely when the Service Team has implemented new applications or platforms.

Continuous deployment

Continuous deployment including promotion from development to production environments.

The continuous deployment approach should continue and evolve throughout the Public Beta.

Timing

This interaction should carry over from earlier in the Beta and so will have already started at the beginning of the Public Beta.
